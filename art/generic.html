"Try to see it. Not with your eyes, for they are wise. But see it with your ears: The cool green breathing of the leaves. And hear it with the inside of your hand: The soundless sound of shadows flicking light. Celebrate sensation. Recall that secret place. You've been there, you remember. That special place where once - just once - in your crowded sunlit lifetime. You hid away in shadows from the tyranny of time. That spot beside the clover. Where someone's hand held your hand."

El Gallo's monologue, The Fantasticks by Tom Jones and Harvey Schmidt

Muses

Landscape

I fully embrace the dynamic nature of landscape and aim to capture the lasting invariants of the hour to yield the essence of place and time such that these moments may forever dry on the canvas and remain fresh in memory.

Figure

In contrast witht the dynamic setting of landscape, figurative works conceived in a controlled environment i.e. the studio afford a more imaginative approach and may be conceptually motivated.

Jaime Luna is a landscape and figure painter residing in the Greater Sacramento Area.
A longtime sketch artist, Jaime took his first oil painting class in 2017 and continued taking painting classes at the University of California, Davis while earning a B.S. in Applied Mathematics.
His introduction to plein air painting in 2021 has been instrumental in developing his compositional skills with form and color.
